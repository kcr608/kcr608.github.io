Nutrition Guide

Nutrition Philosophy

This nutrition plan is designed to support your training program with meals that are:

  • Cost-Effective: Budget-friendly ingredients without sacrificing nutrition
  • Time-Efficient: Quick to prepare with minimal cooking time
  • Performance-Focused: Properly timed macronutrients for optimal training results
  • Recovery-Enhanced: Ingredients that support muscle repair and joint health

Weekly Meal Prep Guide

Save time and money by preparing these key components once per week:

Protein Prep

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 2 lbs lean ground turkey
  • 2 dozen eggs

Preparation:

  1. Season and bake chicken breasts at 375°F for 25-30 minutes
  2. Cook ground turkey with basic seasonings (salt, pepper, garlic)
  3. Hard boil 1 dozen eggs, keep the rest raw
  4. Store in separate containers in refrigerator (3-4 days) or freezer (longer storage)
Carbohydrate Prep

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups uncooked rice (white or brown)
  • 4-6 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 cups uncooked quinoa

Preparation:

  1. Cook rice according to package directions
  2. Bake sweet potatoes at 400°F for 45-60 minutes
  3. Cook quinoa according to package directions
  4. Store in separate containers in refrigerator
Vegetable Prep

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 lbs mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, peppers, etc.)
  • 2 bags pre-washed greens for salads
  • 1 container cherry tomatoes
  • 2-3 onions and garlic bulbs for flavoring

Preparation:

  1. Wash, chop, and roast vegetables with olive oil at 400°F for 20-25 minutes
  2. Store in containers with paper towel to absorb moisture
  3. Keep some vegetables raw for quick snacks
Quick Snack Prep

Ingredients:

  • Protein powder (1-2 containers)
  • Greek yogurt (large container)
  • Natural nut butter
  • Oats
  • Honey
  • Frozen berries

Preparation:

  1. Portion greek yogurt into serving containers
  2. Make protein energy bites and store in refrigerator
  3. Prepare smoothie packets with frozen ingredients (minus liquid)
