Training Program

1.5-Mile Run Optimization & Overall Physical Preparation

Program Overview

This 16-week training program integrates strength training with calisthenics in an AM/PM split format. The program emphasizes CNS development, joint strengthening, and optimizing 1.5-mile run performance.

Each day includes specific focus areas with workouts tailored to develop strength, endurance, and mental toughness. The program follows a carefully structured weekly routine that balances:

  • Maximum strength development (lower body, push, pull)
  • Cardio progression (track speed work, distance runs, recovery cardio)
  • Swimming and treading skill development
  • Breath control training (using the app)
  • Proper recovery protocols
