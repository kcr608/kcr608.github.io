Training Philosophy

Integrated Approach

This program combines strength work with calisthenics in "complexes" that better simulate the body for needed demands. Rather than isolating movements, exercises are paired to develop performance under fatigue.

CNS Development

  • Neural drive enhancement through maximum recruitment exercises
  • Rate of force development training
  • Motor unit synchronization via complex movement patterns
  • Performance maintenance under accumulated fatigue

Joint Development Focus

Instead of just preventing injuries, this program actively strengthens joints through:

  • Progressive loading of connective tissues
  • End-range strength development
  • Multi-directional stability training
  • Proprioceptive challenge progressions

Cardio Focus

Daily cardio is programmed to optimize 1.5-mile run performance:

  • Tuesday: Track circuit for speed development
  • Thursday: 3-mile run for endurance building
  • Saturday: Long run for aerobic base development
  • Other days: Recovery cardio
